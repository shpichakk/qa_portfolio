**Scenarios**

**Scenario 1 \- Through an user’s account’s photo icon on the “Profile” screen**

Given \- User is logged into Instagram and Facebook accounts

And \- User’s Instagram and Facebook accounts are connected

And \- “Your Instagram story” toggle is turned off by default

And \- Instagram’s access to the user’s gallery is open (fully or partially)

When \- User taps his Instagram account’s user photo on the “Profile” screen

Then \- “Add to story” screen is opened

When \- User chooses desirable content for the story and tap on it

Then \-  The chosen content is opened in a “Creating a new story” screen

When \- User taps an “→” icon in the lower right corner of the “Creating a new story” screen

Then \- “Share” bottom sheet menu is opened

When \- User taps “Your story” radio button in the “Share” bottom sheet menu

Then \- “Share to Facebook” popup menu is opened

When \- User taps “Share your story” option at the “Share to Facebook” popup menu

Then \- Text under the radio button “Your story” on the “Share” bottom sheet menu is changed from “Sharing options” to “And Facebook story”

When \- User taps a “Share” button at the bottom of the “Share” bottom sheet menu

Then \- “Also share to” bottom sheet menu is opened

When \- User taps a “Done” button at the bottom of the “Also share to” bottom sheet menu

Then \- The story is posted on the user’s Instagram and Facebook accounts at the same time

**Scenario 7 \- Automatic sharing**

Given \- User is logged into Instagram and Facebook accounts

And \- User’s Instagram and Facebook accounts are connected

And \- “Your Instagram story” toggle is turned off by default

When \- User taps a hamburger icon “☰” in the upper right corner of the “Profile” account’s screen

Then \- “Settings and activity” screen is opened

When \- User taps “Crossposting” option at the “Who can see your content” section

Then \- “Crossposting” screen is opened

When \- User taps an “Accounts Center” option

Then \- “Sharing across profiles” popup menu is opened

When \- User turns on a “Your Instagram story” toggle

Then \- “Your Instagram story” toggle is switched on

And \- User’s new Instagram stories are crossposted on the user’s Facebook account automatically
