### **Bug reports**

### **BR01. An "Accounts Center" menu isn’t functional on the "Crossposting" screen after tapping the "Accounts Center" option**

Environment:

* iPhone 12 Pro, iOS 26.1  
* Instagram app 408.1.0.45.70

Preconditions:

* User is logged into Instagram and Facebook accounts  
* User’s Instagram and Facebook accounts are connected  
* User is on the “Profile” screen of his Instagram account

Steps to reproduce:

1. Tap a “☰” hamburger icon on the “Profile” screen  
2. Tap a “Crossposting” option at the “Who can see your content” section  
3. Tap an “Accounts Center” option

✅Expected result:

“Accounts Center" menu is opened and functional

❌Actual result:

* “Sharing across profiles” menu is opened after tapping an “Accounts Center” option  
* "Accounts Center" menu is visible for 1 second between transition from a “Crossposting” screen to a “Sharing across profiles” menu  
* “Accounts Center” menu isn’t functional

Priority: Low

Severity: Low

### **BR02. Some Nova Post's branches and parcel lockers aren’t displayed in the selection list during the search**

Environment:

* iPhone 12 Pro, iOS 26.1  
* Silpo app 5.14.0

Preconditions:

* User is logged into Silpo app  
* User added some items to cart  
* User is on the “Оформлення замовлення” screen 

Steps to reproduce:

1. Choose a “Нова пошта” delivery method  
2. Tap an “Обрати адресу доставки” button  
3. Tap a “Вкажіть населений пункт” input field  
4. Enter the location of some Nova Post’s branch or parcel locker in the “Вкажіть населений пункт” input field  
5. Select the needed settlement from the list  
6. Enter the number of the Nova Post’s branch or parcel locker in the “Відділення або поштомат” input field

✅Expected result:

User can choose any working Nova Post’s branch or parcel locker for delivery

❌Actual result:

* Some of Nova Post’s branches and parcel lockers aren’t displayed   
* An error message is displayed

Priority: Medium

Severity: Low
